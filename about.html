This type of massage can help: - Relieve tension and pain in the calves - Improve circulation and reduce swelling - Ease muscle cramps and spasms - Enhance flexibility and range of motion - Promote relaxation and reduce stress
Foot massages target the feet, toes, and ankles, providing relaxation and relief. Benefits include: - Relieving tension and pain in the feet and ankles - Improving circulation and reducing swelling - Easing stress and promoting relaxation - Soothing soreness and discomfort - Exfoliating and softening skin
